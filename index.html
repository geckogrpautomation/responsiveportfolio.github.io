Personal Bio

I grew up in the SE of Melbourne. After leaving school I started an electrical appreticeship. For most of my training I was building and assisting in designing control systems, water dosing systems, industrial telemtry & networks plus remote access solutions. I cut my teeth here and after my initial ,certificate was completed I went on to complete a further certificate in automation. After doing many industry based PLC and SCADA courses and a lot of on the job coding experience I decided to complete some more formalised training in SQL databases. I also have completed TCP/IP network training on Layer 2 and Layer 3 integration to provide low latency networks for industrial machinery needing these types of speeds, including robotics, motions control systems and any real time based requirements.

In order to remain current and to live my motto of life long learning; I now here on my way to complete the Monash Bootcamp Program.
